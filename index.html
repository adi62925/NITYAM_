About NITYAM

Welcome to NITYAM by Nayak Agency, an authorized distributor for leading FMCG brands in Madhubani, Bihar. We hold direct distributorships with major companies including Parle, Bikaji, Adani Wilmer, Cargill (Nature Fresh), and Gyan Ghee.

Our mission is to provide high-quality branded products to wholesalers and retailers throughout the Madhubani region, ensuring reliable supply and competitive pricing.

What sets us apart is our direct relationship with manufacturers, ensuring authenticity, freshness, and the best possible pricing for our business customers. We pride ourselves on timely delivery, excellent customer service, and maintaining strong relationships with both our suppliers and customers.

How It Works

1. Contact Us

Reach out to us via phone, email, or by visiting our office in Madhubani. Our sales team will provide you with our current product catalog and pricing information.

2. Place Your Order

Select the products and quantities you need from our extensive range of brand offerings. Orders can be placed through our sales representatives, by phone, or in person at our office.

3. Payment

Choose from multiple payment options including credit terms for regular customers, UPI, bank transfers, or cash on delivery for eligible orders.

4. Delivery

Receive your order at your doorstep within 24-48 hours. Our efficient logistics network ensures timely delivery across Madhubani and surrounding areas.
